This Dog is Awesome

One of the most intelligent, loyal, and dedicated dog breeds out there, the Blue Heeler stands ahead of the pack. Bred for herding cattle in the harsh Australian Outback, this stubborn breed loves to work, complete tasks, and train. They make great hiking companions, agility champions, farm dogs, and are truly mans best friend.

General Information
Herding, is what this breed was made for, they are tough as nails, and always get the job done.
Hiking comes naturally to a heeler, they will venture up the trail, but always stay in sight, and check in regularly.
Play comes in many different forms, brain games are best for tiring out even the smartest Heeler.
Adventure is this breeds middle name, take them anywhere you want to go, and theyll be happy just to be there.
